Chris Webster & Nina Gerber

Sunday, February 8

The Aromas Community Grange is proud to have Chris Webster and Nina Gerber back for an evening of original songs, tasteful covers, soulful ballads, jazzy tunes, and gorgeous folk.

Perhaps you heard about their first appearance - maybe you were there. Either way, you won't want to miss their second benefit concert for the Aromas Grange Hall, at the corner of Rose and Bardue Avenues.

Music starts at 4:30pm - doors open at 3:30 for our renowned chicken dinner (sold separately).

$18 in Advance ~ $20 at the Door
Tickets at Marshall's Market in Aromas & at brownwpapertickets.com

www.aromasgrange.com or 831.254.2444 or 831.726.3609
